The Future of Education Starts After 10th

What if students didn't just study? What if they built ideas, worked on real projects, met founders, and developed leadership while continuing their education?

The Problem

Today most students follow the same path. PU College → Coaching → Exams. But after years of studying many students still lack: confidence communication ability real-world exposure leadership skills problem solving ability Education focuses only on marks.

The Solution

We are building a structured founder ecosystem where students continue their academics while also developing real-world thinking, leadership skills and entrepreneurial exposure. Students don't just study. They build, think, experiment and grow.
